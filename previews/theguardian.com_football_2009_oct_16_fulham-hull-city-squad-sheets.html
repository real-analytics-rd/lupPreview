Squad sheets: Fulham v Hull City

Fri 16 Oct 2009 16.52 BST

With Fulham having already played 14 games this season, and now facing a midweek visit to Roma, Roy Hodgson will have been glad of the rest the international break provided for some of his squad. Phil Brown's first ever top-flight victory came over Fulham, but a lot has changed since then. Despite a much-needed victory over Wigan, Hull still sit in the bottom three and will need to shore up the worst defensive record in the Premier League if they are to avoid the relegation worries that engulfed them last season. Patrick Curry
